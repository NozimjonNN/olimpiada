One of the largest consumer goods manufacturers, implementing sustainable production strategies, including waste reduction, the use of renewable resources, and minimizing environmental impact.

A company producing plant-based meat alternatives, helping reduce the environmental impact of the meat industry, including lowering CO2 emissions and water consumption.
